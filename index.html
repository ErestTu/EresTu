Customer experience

Experiences I made 
By Ella

"Eres Tú has been with me during some special moments. I wore the perfume while graduating, on my first date with my boyfriend and on the day I moved out of my childhood home. Now every morning I put it on and I feel it is going to be a good day and know good things are coming my way."

Easy getting testers
By Monica

"I have a very hard time finding new perfume. I do not have the time to go into the city and try on all the perfumes in the store. Being able to get testers send home and smell them in my own space was such a nice experience. Eres Tú has made it possible for me to pick a perfume from my own home, saving me a lot of time."

Very sustainable
By Jasmijn

I love how sustainable Eres Tú is. When my perfume bottle is empty I never know what to do with the bottle. Sometimes I just keep them in a drawer and sometimes I just throw them away. Now when I want to continue wearing the same perfume but the bottle is empty I can just get it refilled, making that I am not left with an empty bottle.
