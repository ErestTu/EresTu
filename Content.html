Vision

“We live in a rapidly changing world, where we believe that taking a break is the best approach to make new experiences.” 

We as a brand want our audience to make many new memories. Having fun is one of the most important things in life. During these moments, we as a brand want to be there. Wear ‘Eres Tú’ with new experiences.


 

Values

Our brand values experiences, growth, sustainability, and inclusion. 

EXPERIENCES 

We want to be there with our customers while they are making new life experiences. 

GROWTH 

We want our customers to be able to grow with us and for us to grow with our customers. 

SUSTAINABILITY 

We include sustainability in our products. When the perfume bottle is empty it can be send back and refilled. 

INCLUSION 

We want our brand to include all genders and have no exclusion.
