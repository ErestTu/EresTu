Japan

Visit futuristic cities and traditional shrines in the land of the rising sun!

  • Fly direct from Schiphol to Tokyo-Narita or Kansai International.
  • Visit Tokyo and its surroundings.
  • From noodle soups and train-station lunchboxes to fine traditional dining, enjoy the diversity of one of the world's greatest cuisines.
  • Hop on the Shinkansen (bullet train) to Kyoto, Nara and Osaka.
  • If you have extra time, we will arrange visits to the sacred island of Miyajima and the Peace Memorial in Hiroshima.

Việt Nam

  • From Phở soup to Bánh mì sandwiches, enjoy truly amazing food at every street corner.
  • Discover the magical islets of the Hạ Long Bay.
  • Trek around mountains and rice paddies in Sapa.
