About us


 

Brand Info

We are a new brand created in 2022. Eres Tú was build on the vision that we live in a rapidly changing world, where we believe that taking a break is the best approach to make new experiences. Our brand values making experiences, growth, inclusion and sustainability. We want to be there with our customers while they are making new life experiences and be able to grow with them and them being able to grow with us. Our brand strives to include all people in our products and making sure no one is excluded.  


 

Product & services

Eres Tú sells perfume. We strive to create a large scale of various perfumes, making that there is one that fits everyone. For our products we work very hard to be sustainable. When your perfume bottle is empty, instead of buying a new bottle you can get it refilled with a discount!
