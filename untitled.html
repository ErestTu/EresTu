Marketing

Context of campaign & promotional activities

Our brand is created on the vision: “We live in a rapidly changing world, where we believe that taking a break is the best approach to make new experiences”. Our brand values making experiences, growth and sustainability. We as a brand want to be there with our customer while they are making new life experiences, be able to grow with our customers and for them to be able to grow with us, and include sustainability in our products. We are conveying this to our target audience by using Instagram and TikTok. The reach objective we have for Instagram is 50-100 followers and 50-150 amount of accounts reached and for TikTok 100-200 views per post. The affect objective is that we want our target audience to get a feel of our brand. We want them to understand our values and creative vision and style from our posts. The response objective we have for Instagram is that we want our target audience to see our post, go to our account and get a understanding of our brand and start following us. For TikTok we want our target audience to see our post, go to our account and look at our other post and get a understanding of our brand. We chose Instagram and TikTok because they are the most used social media platforms by our target audience and because we believe they give us a good opportunity to showcase who we are as a brand. Instagram and TikTok give us the opportunity to post personalized content that is in line with our brand. These platforms also use algorithms based on their users behaviour, making that we are able to reach accounts that do not follow us yet, but are interested in similar content as the content we create. 


 

Learning points

Overall we believe this project went well. As we expected we really feel like we were able to communicate our values and creative vision and style to our audience. The content we created and posted was able to convey our message. Our reach objective however was not fully achieved. We were able to reach the number of Instagram followers that we wanted and for some of the Instagram post we reached our goals. The first couple of post reached the amount of account we wanted to reach, but then it went down and we were not able to reach our goal. The same happened with TikTok, it started out really good with us reaching our goal and then went down. If we could do it differently we would look further into how we can keep our numbers and what is the best way to do so. The Instagram and TikTok algorithm did as we wanted help us to get our content shown to accounts that did not follow us yet. From the statistics per post we were able to see that around half of the people who saw our post did not follow us yet. .


 

Future planning

If we would continue the project we would continue making content in the style we have chosen. We feel like the style that we decided on the create our content for the media platforms is the best way to show our brand. We would also continue with our content planning, since it helped us have a structure and made sure we did not post too much or not enough. We would recommend ourselves to look further into how to keep our account numbers going, because in the end our numbers went down. We would also look even more into the algorithms of the media platforms, because we now only looked at the surface of what they were but we believe they can do way more for us then what they did now.
